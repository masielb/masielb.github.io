About Me

A little bit about myself.

My name is Masiel Bautista and I am a full-stack developer based in Durham, North Carolina. I recently graduated from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill where I studied Global Economics, Trade, and Development as well as Hispanic Linguistics. I have always enjoyed learning about the world around me and meeting people of diverse backgrounds. Modern-day technology has played a major role in my ability to interact with other parts of the world.

I made the switch to computer programming when I noticed how web development and design not only engaged with my previous studies, but also allowed me to express my creative side. I am very detail-oriented and I love to work on projects end to end, from ideation all the way through development. For that reason, I decided to become a full-stack developer after completing my undergraduate career.
